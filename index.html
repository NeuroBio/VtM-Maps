The Game

Phoenix Heat was a two story, 32 session Vampire the Masquerade (a TTRPG) campaign I ran from 2020-2021. The game focused on player characters Thaddeus (a fledgling Tremere) and Izzuddin (Iz, a fledgling Malkavain) and the unstable politics within and between the fictional city Hartstone and Phoenix. This reference website marks out key locations in the game (events and Kindred/Vampire havens) as well as gives quick bios on the two player characters and the myriad of NPCs I commanded as the Storyteller (game master).
